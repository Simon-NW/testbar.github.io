I am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Computer Science at the University of Copenhagen. Before that, I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science at the University of Bonn, working with the groups of Heiko Röglin and Anne Driemel. My main research interests are algorithms and complexity, specifically on approximation algorithms and computational geometry. During my studies, I spent an exchange semester at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver and am honored to be supported by the Studienstiftung des deutschen Volkes scholarship.
